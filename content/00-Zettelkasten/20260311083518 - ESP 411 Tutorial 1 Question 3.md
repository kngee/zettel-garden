You are told that an analog signal has a uniform power spectral density and is bandlimited with a filter that has a given amplitude response. $$|H(f)|=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+(\frac{f}{f_c})^6}}$$
Our goal is to digitize this signal with a linear 8-bit ADC. The goals is to determine the minimum sampling frequency so the maximum aliasing error is less than the quantization error level in the passband.
