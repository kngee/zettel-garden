You are looking at a real DSP system and told that it has a 16-bit ADC configured in bipolar mode. The peak-to-peak amplitude of the input signal is $\pm 10~V$ and sits in the $0-10~ kHz$ band. There are a number of examples in the textbook that provide the various scenarios required to be able to answer this question.

This question asks us to determine the minimum stopband attenuation $A_{min}$ for the anti-aliasing filter. Then we need to find the sampling frequency required to keep the aliasing error in the passband below the quantization noise level.
