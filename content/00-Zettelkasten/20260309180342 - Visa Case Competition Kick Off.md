> What is a CVP
- Credit Card CVP that looks for the best offerings for the client that is profitable and shows scalable economics
- You are in South Africa, it is already a large bank. Looking to differentiate itself from the competition.
- Use market dynamics to show customer direction. Leverage new ways that card is accepted through fintechs

1. Research innovative Credit Card CVP (globally), its features, fees and rewards
2. CVP benchmarking, identify the key components and benchmark them.

- It should answer why a customer should choose this card over others. 

> Why a bank would want a new CVP?

1. Reduction in customer acquisition, so they address new needs more efffectively.
2. Lack differentiation, so they make a new product to gain a competitive advantage.
3. If there is a shift in consumer preferences, need to remain relevant
4. Man profitability, it needs to be refreshed to drive revenue. It needs to ensure that the product is product not just for the next 2 years but for an extended period of time.
