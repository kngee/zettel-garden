> Project Proposal discussion lecture in EPR 402 
---
- Due date is 21 April but remember that Dr. Paluncic wants the first draft by 24 March.
- Use the full project number, ie FP1
- Submitted on the 21st of April is *Rev 0*. We are allowed a maximum of 2 revisions
- It is possible to have a language editor review the quality of the language and grammer in the submitted project proposal.
- Page 1 project description should just use non technical language, feel free to use the project concept note. The focus is on what and why, is it clear why its necessary and what the end product looks like.
- We can include any picture that caputes how I visualize the final product! It doesn't need to tell us that much about the project.![[Pasted image 20260303145238.png|center]]
- The tick boxes on page 2 are to ensure that we understand that all processing will need to be done on an embedded platform. Also that library functions will be used for hardware interfacing only.
- Once you are able to put the project into your own words you can begin thinking about a functional block diagram.
- The functional block diagram and descriptions are not expected to be your final design that you will be evaluated against.
- Write all the requirements before moving onto the specification
- Requirements focus on what the system should do and the specification focuses on how well it should be done, think about it in terms of how well an objective needs to be met.
- Its not about what you think you are able to achieve but rather what you need to achieve in order to solve the problem.
- In the third rank you need to explain why the specification is the target, ie. why this particular number/value for the specification.
- The fourth rank is concerned we will demonstrate to prove that the requirements are met.
- The 5th rank is concerned with what our own contribution is as in what will we do from first principles. 
- The final rank should include items, such as elecotrnonic components, embedded hardplatform or library function. 
- Note there is a section for field conditions
- Section 6 handles any new things that we need to learn that need to be included into the project proprosal.
