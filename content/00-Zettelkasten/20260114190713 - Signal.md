In the general sense, a *signal* is anything (physical, digital, symbolic) that has the ability to retain and communicate information or data. 

>Whether it is in financial markets, electronic systems, telecommunication systems or much much more, what I am learning is that the way we ought to make sense of and preserve the integrity of signals is much the same.

In our study of telecommunications, our signal of interest was a continuous function, or atleast that is how it is described to be at this stage. I believe this to be representative of a message that was modulated and transmitted as a voltage.
