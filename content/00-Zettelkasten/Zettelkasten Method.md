> The function of Zettelkasten is to allow for emergent relationships to take place between your ideas.

> [!note]
> The workflow is simple: **Capture, Process, Connect**. *More time should be spent connecting notes rather than making them*
> 

- While studying or in a lecture, we make quick capture with the purpose of capturing a piece of information as it was given to me by either the lecturer or the textbook.
- Soon after the studying, we work to clarify any of the misunderstandings from the capturing phase by creating a permanent that explains a core concept. We focus on making connections once we have explained the idea.

### Fleeting Notes
For an idea to warrant being a fleeting note, it needs to meet at least one of these criteria:
1. You want to remember the idea permanently
2. You want to use the idea in your work.
